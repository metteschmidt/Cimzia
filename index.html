Warfarin Limitations can Leave Many Patients Unprotected

Anticoagulation effectively prevents strokes, yet
Warfarin drawbacks can challenge even your best efforts5

    anticoagulation
  • Frequent dose
    adjustments
  • Coagulation monitoring
  • Many drug-drug
    interactions
  • Dietary restrictions
  • Patient adherence

no anticoagulation or inadequate anticoagulation

    anticoagulated at the time of first stroke4

ROCKET AF is a pivotal,
double-blind study with over
14,000 AF patients from
1,100 sites across
45 countries with a
2-year median follow-up11

ROCKET AF is a Rigorous,
Double-Blind Stroke Prevention Trial11,12

aEnrollment of patients without prior stroke, transient ischaemic attack (TIA), or systemic embolism and only 2 factors capped at 10%

ROCKET AF is a Rigorous,
Double-Blind Stroke Prevention Trial11,12

Primary outcome measure

  • A composite of stroke and systemic embolism (SE)11

Secondary outcome measures

  • A composite of stroke, SE, and vascular death11
  • A composite of stroke, SE, myocardial infarction (MI),
    and vascular death11

Principal safety outcome measure

  • A composite of major and non-major clinically relevant
    (NMCR) bleeding events11
