ROCKET AF is a Rigorous,
Double-Blind Stroke Prevention Trial11,12

Primary outcome measure

  • A composite of stroke and systemic embolism (SE)11

Secondary outcome measures

  • A composite of stroke, SE, and vascular death11
  • A composite of stroke, SE, myocardial infarction (MI),
    and vascular death11

Principal safety outcome measure

  • A composite of major and non-major clinically relevant
    (NMCR) bleeding events11
